About plumeria

Get rewarded for learning!

Plumeria ESL is a tutoring center, which fulfills and focuses on the needs of the ESL learner. Whether you are a young learner or an adult learner, our teachers are there to help you succeed in learning English. We offer both one-to-one sessions on specific days (please refer to our "rates page"), but we also invite students to join our small group sessions, which helps enhance speaking and listening skills.

We begin with a brief test to help us define your level and then immediately begin your English session!Our young learners program is filled with creative activities, in order to keep the child motivated and enthusiastic, while building their vocabulary, reading and writing skills. Adult learners are also taken on a fun adventure while learning, since they are exposed to multiple tasks, enhancing and building reading, writing, listening and speaking skills in the classroom!

Plumeria is a flower that is capable of growing in different areas of the world, including Central America, Mexico, Brazil, South America and the Caribbean. What does this mean to Plumeria ESL? Well, the Plumeria flower is like an English learner; wherever you come from, you are capable of growing and striving for excellence in the English language! We are here to make your learning experience, one to remember!
